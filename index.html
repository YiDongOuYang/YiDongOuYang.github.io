About

Hey, I am Yidong Ouyang, a Ph.D. candidate at UCLA, where I am advised by George Michailidis. Prior to this, I earned my MPhil from CUHK Shenzhen, where I was advised by Liyan Xie. My research interests lie in generative models, especially diffusion and flow models.
